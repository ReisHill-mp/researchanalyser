Executive summary
Responses to "imagine you want to quickly add some important occasions so moonpig can remind you about them in future. what would you expect this kind of feature to let you do?" were mixed, with several participants sharing improvement ideas or unmet expectations.

Participants generally responded positively to "if you could change one thing about this experience, what would it be and why?". The strongest reactions mentioned clarity, ease, or a smoother-than-expected experience.

This group showed more friction signals, including hesitation, confusion, or slower progress through the task.
